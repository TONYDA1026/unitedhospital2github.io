VALUES

United Hospitals PLC understands the dynamic and urgent nature of the healthcare sector, which has evolved over the decades from a reactive to a proactive service. In this rapidly changing environment, Nawaloka Hospital medical and support teams have identified five strategic areas to focus and invest time and resources.

united Hospital Branches

Central Branch

Branch 01 is our flagship location, offering a comprehensive range of medical services. From routine check-ups to advanced treatments, we are equipped to handle diverse healthcare needs. Our modern facilities and dedicated staff ensure high-quality care in a convenient and accessible setting.

East Health Hub

Branch 02 specializes in emergency and inpatient care, providing round-the-clock support for urgent medical needs. Our skilled emergency care team is available 24/7, and our inpatient services offer a comfortable environment for extended care. We are dedicated to delivering prompt and effective care whenever you need it most.

North Wing

Branch 03 focuses on outpatient services and minor procedures. We offer efficient, high-quality care without the need for an overnight stay. Our dedicated team ensures a seamless experience, from diagnosis to treatment, with a focus on patient comfort and convenience.
